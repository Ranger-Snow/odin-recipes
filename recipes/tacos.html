Tacos

Description

These tacos are made with fresh ingredients and a blend of spices for a flavorful meal.

Ingredients

  • Taco shells
  • Ground beef
  • Taco seasoning
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Shredded cheese
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add taco seasoning and a bit of water, stir and simmer until thickened.
  3. Warm the taco shells in the oven or on a skillet.
  4. Assemble the tacos by filling each shell with beef, lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and sour cream.
  5. Serve immediately.
