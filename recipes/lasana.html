Lasagna

Description

A classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, cheese, and meat sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Cheese
  • Meat Sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil pasta according to package instructions.
  3. Layer pasta, cheese, and sauce in a baking dish.
  4. Bake for 25 minutes or until bubbly and golden.
