Pizza

Description

This homemade pizza is topped with fresh ingredients and a savory tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Bell peppers

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the dough to the desired thickness.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Add toppings, starting with cheese, followed by pepperoni and vegetables.
  5. Bake for 10-15 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
